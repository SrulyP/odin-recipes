Banana Oat Bread

Description

A delicious, healthy banana oat bread recipe perfect for getting toddlers to eat some fruit and oats without a fuss. This recipe is also perfect for those who are gluten-free or gluten-intolerant and still want a baked good.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups instant or quick-oats
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 large bananas
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • ½ cup chopped chocolate
  • ¼th cup chia seeds (optional for more fiber)
  • ¼th cup honey (optional for sweetness, I omit this)
  • 1 tsp baking soda

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Blend the oats, eggs, honey if using any, and bananas in a blender until smooth.
  3. Place the mixture in a mixing bowl, and mix in the chia seeds, baking soda, walnuts, and chocolate.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased baking dish.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes or until you can insert a toothpick and it comes out clean.
  6. Cool for 10-20 minutes and enjoy.
